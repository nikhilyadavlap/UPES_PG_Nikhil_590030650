## 📌 Overview

The **Research** section is designed to help students move from a broad academic interest to a clear, structured, and convincing research output.

This folder covers two connected learning tracks:

1. **Research Proposal Writing**  
   Learn how to identify a research problem, review literature, define research gaps, frame objectives, design methodology, and prepare a complete academic research proposal.

2. **Pitch Your Ideas**  
   Learn how to convert an idea into a clear pitch by identifying a problem, understanding constraints, defining users, explaining value, and presenting the idea with impact.
